# 🦌 Buck

> **Your invisible AI writing assistant — right inside any app.**

Buck lives quietly in the background as an Android Accessibility Service. While you type in **any text field** on your phone, just drop a `@` and Buck kicks in: it grabs your text, sends it to an AI model, and **streams back a clean, reformatted version** — token by token — directly into the field you're typing in.

No copy-paste. No app switching. No friction.

---

### ⚡ How to use

1. **Open Buck** and tap the button to enable the Accessibility Service.
2. **Go to any app** — WhatsApp, Notes, Chrome, whatever.
3. **Type your messy text** and add `@` anywhere in it.
4. **Watch it rewrite itself** in real time, word by word.

---

### 🧠 Why Buck?

Sometimes you just need to fire off a quick message but your brain dumps a wall of text. Buck turns that into something short and clean — **without leaving the app you're in**.
